About us

Intrig Sureway Insurance Agency Ltd was registered in 2002 to transact all classes of insurance. We have complied with all the requirements of the Insurance RegulatoryAuthority [IRA], the regulatory body of the Insurance Industry. Our licence number is IRA/05/2185 which expires on 31 st December 2019. Sureway Insurance Agency Ltd is a market oriented and service driven organisation that is constantly responding to the needs of insurance consumers. We provide superior products from the leading insurance companies, which have an impeccable claim paying ability and record.
